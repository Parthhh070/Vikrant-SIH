Floral Waste Cleaning

Vikrant’s advanced technology helps remove floral waste efficiently from lakes, preserving the natural beauty of water bodies. By tackling this environmental concern, we ensure that lakes remain healthy and free from organic waste, promoting aquatic life and preventing water contamination.

Water Hyacinth Removal

Water hyacinths can choke lakes and block sunlight, harming aquatic ecosystems. Vikrant is specifically designed to clear large patches of water hyacinth, improving water quality and restoring the balance of the ecosystem. we help bring life back to your water bodies by removing these invasive plants swiftly and effectively.

Plastic Waste Collection

Plastic waste poses a serious threat to lakes, wildlife, and ecosystems. Vikrant’s robust waste collection system is equipped to efficiently gather plastics from the water, ensuring a cleaner, safer environment. By removing plastic debris, we help prevent pollution and reduce the harmful effects of plastic on nature.
