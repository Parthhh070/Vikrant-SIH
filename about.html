our story

Debugging Earth: The Journey Behind Vikrant

Our journey began a year ago with a shared vision: to combat water pollution and restore the beauty of our lakes. The idea of tackling environmental challenges with innovative technology inspired us, and from this spark, our team name—*Debugging Earth*—was born. We embraced this mission wholeheartedly, spending countless hours brainstorming, testing, and refining our ideas. Through tireless debugging and unwavering determination, Vikrant came to life: an intelligent, efficient, and sustainable solution to tackle the growing threat of lake pollution. Today, as Vikrant makes a tangible impact, we look back with pride on how *Debugging Earth* turned a dream into reality—and our journey is only just beginning.

Together, we believe in creating a cleaner, greener future—one lake at a time. With Vikrant and the spirit of Debugging Earth, we are committed to turning challenges into opportunities and inspiring change for generations to come. Let’s restore the Earth, starting with our lakes!
